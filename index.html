Certified Financial Advisors vs Planners vs Consultants: Which Do You Need?

Certified Financial Advisor

Financial advisors offer a variety of services. It is a term much broader in scope.

A financial advisor may or may not be certified.

Certified Financial Planner

The main focus of a financial planner is to help you achieve short-, mid-, and long-term financial goals through detailed plans.

A financial planner can be certified, like CFPs who gain certification by experience and passing exams set by the Financial Planning Standards Board.

Certified Financial Consultant

Financial consultants offer highly specialized expert guidance that is more suited to the short term.

A financial consultant may or may not be certified.
